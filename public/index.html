Welcome To My Little Bit Of The Web


Learning Web Development

In late 2016 I started working on FreeCodeCamp's Front End course in my spare time, and I made this website to document my progress. Through the course I became familiar with APIs, Jquery, and manipulating the DOM.

Once I completed the Front End section of the course, I decided to learn Back End and enrolled in Andrew Mead's course on Node.js on Udemy, which familiarised me with Express, Mongoose, and MongoDB. Upon completing the course in January, I started applying what I learned to my old front end projects, setting them up with Node and then deploying them with Heroku.

Now that I'm comfortable with the basics of Full Stack Development, I'm working on some personal projects(webscraper viewable on github) and enrolled in a REACT course on Udemy.
